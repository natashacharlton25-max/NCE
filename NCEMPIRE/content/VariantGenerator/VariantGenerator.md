> **Type:** Content Module
> **Status:** Placeholder

## Overview

VariantGenerator generates multiple variants of the same content with different angles, tones, lengths, or approaches.

---

## Notes

### Role
- Generate tone variants (empathetic vs practical)
- Generate length variants (short/medium/long)
- Generate approach variants (evidence-based vs story-based)
- Track which variant performs best

### Owns
- Variant generation logic
- Variant relationships
- Performance tracking
