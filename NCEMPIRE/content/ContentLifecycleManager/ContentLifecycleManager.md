> **Type:** Content Module
> **Status:** Placeholder

## Overview

ContentLifecycleManager tracks content through entire lifecycle (draft → QA → ready → published → archived → retired).

---

## Notes

### Role
- Track content stage transitions
- Notify when content needs review
- Generate reports (content at each stage)
- Archive old content
- Alert on stuck content

### Owns
- Lifecycle state machine
- Stage notifications
- Lifecycle reports
