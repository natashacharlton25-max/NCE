## Overview

ContentAdapterEngine takes completed content and adapts it for other uses (workbook → social posts, email → blog, blog → video script).

---

## Notes

### Role
- Analyze source content structure
- Map to target format requirements
- Adapt content (remove/add/reorganize)
- Generate format-specific elements
- Track adaptation lineage

### Owns
- Adaptation logic
- Format mapping
- Lineage tracking

### Why High Priority
- Generate 1 piece, repurpose to 5 formats = massive efficiency
