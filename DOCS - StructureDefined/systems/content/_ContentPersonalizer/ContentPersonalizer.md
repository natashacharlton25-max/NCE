> **Type:** Content Module
> **Status:** Placeholder

## Overview

ContentPersonalizer applies audience-specific insights to generated content, adapting tone, vocabulary, examples, complexity, and accessibility.

---

## Notes

### Role
- Adapt tone & voice (clinical → conversational)
- Adapt vocabulary (technical → plain language)
- Adapt examples (corporate vs family)
- Adjust complexity level
- Apply accessibility adjustments

### Owns
- Adaptation logic
- Transformation tracking
- Integrity validation

### Why Critical
- AudienceManager profiles but doesn't apply
- Same content generates for all audiences without this
