## Overview

PublishingOrchestrator coordinates the entire publishing pipeline from finished content to multiple output formats and platforms.

---

## Notes

### Role
- Coordinate multi-format rendering (PDF, DOCX, HTML, Canva, Google Docs)
- Manage upload sequences to platforms
- Handle platform-specific requirements
- Track publication status per platform
- Generate publication reports

### Owns
- Publishing workflow
- Platform uploads
- Publication tracking
- Version management

### Why Critical
- No module currently owns "what happens after content is generated"
- Renderers exist but no orchestration
